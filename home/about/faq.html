Frequently Asked Questions

Questions are good, it's how we learn. Find answers to common questions here.

  • Do I need to know how to code to use these tools?

    No, these are built with the idea of zero-code users in mind.

    Understandably, you might have to do a few quick setup steps. I try to walk you through those as painlessly as possible. Otherwise, once you run the tool, it's quite straightforward.

  • Do I need to pay for any of these?

    No, they're entirely free.

    I didn't start this with the intention of making any money. I started this project with a desire to innovate. Though I dabbled with the idea of starting a ko-fi link, and maybe making enough to just fuel my coffee mug, I decided against it. I'd really just love to network with you. If you like my content and want to show me your support, shoot me a message or an invite.

  • Can I take your code and re-purpose it, or change it?

    Absolutely. You can rework this however you see fit.

    I don't really like paywalls, and I certainly dislike instructors and resources who seemingly scam their users into opening their wallets, only to discover a lackluster trade. You came here for free tools, you get free tools. Use these however they suit you. I hope they help.
