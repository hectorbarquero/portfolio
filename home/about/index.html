Information

This project was made to help people publish and maintain documentation, or perform related functions, such as the presentation of technical data.

It's not just meant for technical writers, but managers, product managers, sales engineers, marketing professionals, and more.

 

FAQ

Here's a few answers to common questions I've had:

  • can you re-purpose these tools? Yes. You can use them however you'd like.

  • how much do you charge? Every tool you see here is free.

  • are you available for hire? Yes, but right now I'm working with Digital Extremes.

  • how do you use these tools? I leave documentation with each one, but generally they're available as .zips and executed from the command line.

  • why are you making these? I like solving problems.

  • do all technical writers make tools and write code? No. That's why there's a need for these I think.

  • do your tools work on Windows and Mac? Yes. Just make sure to check the documentation for any needed dependencies.
